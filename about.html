About

We're building yield infrastructure for the multi-chain future.

mimo was founded on a simple observation: DeFi yield is fragmented across chains, and users shouldn't need to be bridge experts to earn returns.

The problem

Today, a user wanting to earn yield on Ethereum has to manually bridge to Arbitrum, find a lending protocol, supply liquidity, monitor positions, and repeat this across every chain. The result: most capital stays on Ethereum earning 2-3% while opportunities paying 15%+ exist elsewhere.

Bridges are the #1 hack vector in DeFi — $2.8B lost in 2023 alone. Users shouldn't have to choose between yield and security.

Our approach

mimo uses intent-based architecture. Users express what they want (yield on their deposit) and our solver network figures out how to get there — atomically, across any supported chain, without traditional bridges.

Think of it like this: instead of you navigating a maze of bridges and protocols, you tell mimo "find me the best yield" and it solves the path for you.

Why mimo

The name "mimo" comes from the concept of mimicking — our protocol mimics the behavior of a sophisticated DeFi operator, but does it automatically, privately, and without exposing users to bridge risk.
